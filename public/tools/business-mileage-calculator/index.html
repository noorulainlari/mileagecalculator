Business Mileage Deduction Guide

What Qualifies as Business Mileage?

  • Travel between work locations
  • Client meetings and site visits
  • Business conferences and training
  • Business errands (bank, supplies, etc.)
  • Temporary work assignments

What Does NOT Qualify?

  • Regular commuting to work
  • Personal errands or travel
  • Travel between home and regular workplace
  • Personal use of company vehicle
